Will Pimentel

Dad | Realtor | Lover of The Arts | Tech Enthusiast |

Licensed Pennsylvania Realtor by trade for RPA Real Estate

As a quiet kid who moved around a lot, I was always looking for new ways to express myself.
It started with long drives and loud music, staring out the window and pretending to be the protagonist in the nonexistent music video that I called life.
Before I knew it, that love for music had escalated into creating music of my own. But after a while that wasn't enough...
I needed more. I needed visual ways to express myself. Thats when I found my love for photography, design, and development.
After freelancing for a while I went on to the lead the marketing efforts of a few businesses and entrepreneurs.
Eventually, after having two adorable children, I got my Real Estate license. Getting my real estate license allowed me to take all the things that I was already doing for everyone else, and apply them to my own business.
But despite any of my successes or talents/skills, theres always been something weighing heavy on my heart.
The need to finally become a developer.

Wish me luck
